Welcome to the MEC MH website.

The Men’s hostel of MEC has been an integral part of Govt. Model Engineering College from the very beginning itself. The Men’s hostel and its inmates have traditionally played a pivotal role in almost every activity in the college.

About the history of MH MEC, the Men’s hostel was initially ran in a temporary building at Kaloor, when the college was functioning at Edappally. Later, when the college got a new building at Thrikkakara, a Men’s hostel building was constructed next to the college building for convenience of the students. Construction was completed in stages and students moved in after the completion of the first stage. At present, the hostel is a single block building having three storeys. The hostel houses both undergraduate and postgraduate students. Accommodation is on a room sharing basis.
